About

I'm Tede, a systems engineer based in northern Germany with a background in networking, firewall automation, and data protection infrastructure. I've been building things on the web since I was 13 — starting with hobby sites and eventually turning that curiosity into a career in IT.

I completed a dual study programme in Communications & Media Informatics at Deutsche Telekom, then spent a few years working on network automation projects before joining Veeam in 2023. Currently, I'm a Senior Systems Engineer there, helping customers protect their data and infrastructure.

Outside of work, I enjoy tinkering with technology and this website — which I've been running in various forms since 2014.
